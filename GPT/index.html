Welcome to New Innovations in the World (AI)

This website, created by Lakshit Arora, a class 10th student, explores how Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming our world. The journey through AI's advantages, the potential future, and its impacts will give you an in-depth view of modern technological evolution.
